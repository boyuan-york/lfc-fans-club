About this site

LFC Fan Hub is a small, fan-made website built for the IY4103 Web Development module. I follow Liverpool FC and wanted a simple place to share a few stories ??the stadium, the culture, and what it is like to follow the season ??without pretending to be an official club outlet.

Who it is for

Supporters, curious football fans, and anyone visiting as part of a coursework review. The layout takes inspiration from professional club homepages (card feeds, bold type, honour stats) but stays static and lightweight on purpose.

What you will find

Three short articles linked from the home page, an about page (this one), and a contact form if you would like to say hello. News content is written for this project; it is not pulled from a live wire service.

Disclaimer: This is an unofficial fan project. It is not endorsed by, affiliated with, or operated by Liverpool Football Club. Club names and honours are used descriptively for educational purposes.
